About

Hello, I'm Mathis. Despite being an introvert, I'm open to meet new people, I love creating new projects stimulating my abilities and pushing me over my limits.

You can call me front-end developer, UI, UX designer or whatever you want. Coming from studies related to art before starting programming, UI is very important for me and, as they say, user experience is priority.

Others tend to define my skills and me by the work I've done but I don't like this vision. For me, what defines me the best is that my skills and abilities can easily be adapted to any situation. I never stop learning and every project I do allows me to practise some more.

A whole life wouldn't be enough to use all my energy when it comes to programming and it made me interested in many different subjects. I'm a fast learner, able to pick up new skills and juggle different projects and roles relatively easily.

I like developing things in Javascript and React, which are my favorite languages, but I do not overlook the other languages I have learned so far.
